Sound pack
==========

These sound samples were created by Dubtrain <angband@dubtrain.com> and are
licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International licence
(https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). The originals are available in
WAV format at http://www.dubtrain.com/angband/.

They are bundled here (converted to MP3) as the game's default sound pack. You
may replace them with your own pack by pointing the game at a different base
URL with the ?sounds= query parameter.
